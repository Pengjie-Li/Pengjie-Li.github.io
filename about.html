Achievements

Molecular Structure of 10Be

Using the 10Be(p,pα)6He quasi-free knockout reaction at RIKEN-RIBF, we directly measured the α-cluster wave function in the 10Be ground state. The result gives the clearest evidence to date that nuclei can "clusterize" even in their ground states — a molecule-like 2α+2n structure. PRL (2023), Featured in Physics.

Deeply Bound Orbitals in Ca

Single-particle strengths of deeply bound hole states in neutron-rich Ca isotopes via (p,pn) knockout at RIBF/RIKEN — PLB 855, 138828 (2024).

GET/DAQ Optimization

Optimized the GET electronics system for DSSSD readout with sub-100 ns timing resolution and high dynamic range — NIMA (2026).

Current & Future

Cluster Knockout at SAMURAI

Probing α, t, and non-α cluster correlations in Be, Li, B isotopes via quasi-free knockout reactions (p,pα), (p,pt) at SAMURAI/RIKEN. Ongoing data analysis and upcoming experiments.

Shell Evolution & Unbound States

Invariant-mass spectroscopy of neutron-rich unbound states and single-particle strengths to probe magic-number evolution at the drip line.

HIAF-HIRIBL Campaign

Planned experiments at the new HIAF-HIRIBL facility: neutron-rich nuclei spectroscopy, cluster structure studies, and next-generation detector development.
